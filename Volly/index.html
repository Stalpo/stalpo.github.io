Controls:

use a and d to move left and right (double tap for dash)
use left and right arrows to angle your shots
use the spacebar to jump and s to fast fall
press escape to toggle full screen

Description:

funny physics game about hitting a ball into a purple square!
DO NOT LET IT HIT THE FLOOR!
