What is this Website for?

This website was created for the purpose of putting aspiring game developers projects onto the internet.
I personaly (mr Bones) think that the best way to learn game dev is to get people to play what you make as soon as possible.
For this purpose please enjoy the games knowing that they may not be of the best quality.

Who made the Website / Games?

Since this website is public all people involved in the website have no requirement to release any personal information.
If someone does want to reveal who they are you can find it on their creator page.
